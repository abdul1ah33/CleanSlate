About CleanSlate

CleanSlate helps you remove old and unwanted posts across multiple social media platforms. Whether you want to delete all posts before a specific date or between two dates, CleanSlate makes the process simple and fast.

Features

Multi-Platform Support

Works with Reddit, Twitter, and Discord.

Flexible Date Selection

Choose to delete posts before a specific date or within a date range.

Open Source

Fully open-source with no limitations on deletions.
